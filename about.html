Established in 2021, Fishtail Sports Club is a team of aspiring Nepalese immigrant youth competing in several sports activities that is held in Finland. The name Fishtail is derived from a name of mountain situated in north-central part of Nepal. It is called Machapuchare in Nepali langauge. This mountain has never been officailly climbed hence forming the motto of this team to never be defeated.
